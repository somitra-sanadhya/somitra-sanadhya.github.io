I used to play online chess till few years ago, but no longer active now. However, I try to follow all tournaments that Vishy plays in. Sites I like to follow live chess games are chessbase.in, chess24.com and chess.com 

While I was a faculty member at IIIT Delhi, I was involved in forming "The 65th Square Chess Club" there, along with my former student [Sanchit Gupta](https://www.linkedin.com/in/iamsanchitgupta/). I also created the proposal for bonus marks for FIDE rated Chess players as one of the criteria for bonus marks in the admissions to the institute. It was accepted and I am aware that the scheme of bonus marks for Chess in the admissions was continuining till I worked there. I did not keep track of the place since I left in 2017 and have no idea of the current status.
